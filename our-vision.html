Our Vision

How virtual education should be.

When the COVID-19 virus caused global lockdowns and panic, we - as students ourselves - experienced the shift to virtual and online learning. Classroom experiences were relegated to talking through screens and microphones.

Orchestra and music classes aren't meant to be taught virtually, especially when the teacher has 30 other students to keep track of. On the other hand, paid private lessons can often cost upwards of $50 an hour. We realized how this might discourage or deter new and returning students to continue with their music education.

With this in mind, we founded Verhythm Studio, a youth organization built around our three driving ideals - quality, affordability, and accessibility. We hope that, by offering these free music lessons, we can encourage beginner and returning musicians to continue to further their learning even in these difficult times.
