Verhythm Studio

Online Music Lessons

No fees. No tuition.

We strive to make music accessible, as it should be. That’s why we offer comprehensive, one-hour weekly private lessons in numerous instruments for beginners and intermediate musicians - completely free.

Students are taught by our team of music instructors, all with 8+ years of private instruction and experience. Our teachers are patient, supportive, and passionate about helping their students be the best musicians they can be.

Expert teaching, for free.

One-to-one instruction.

Feel like group classes aren't cutting it? Our lessons are one-to-one, private lessons, in order to make sure you (or your child) receives the best education possible.

We teach students regardless of current musical and technical skills. In fact, we encourage you to pick up a new instrument - you won’t regret it. We do require students to already possess a rented/bought instrument for lessons.

Everyone is welcome.
